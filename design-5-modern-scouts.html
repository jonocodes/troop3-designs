FAQs

Common Questions

When does the pack meet?

Pack 3 meets monthly as a full pack from September through June. Individual dens (grouped by grade) meet more frequently based on den leader schedules.

What does membership cost?

Annual dues cover BSA registration plus pack activities. Financial assistance is available ‚Äî no scout is turned away for inability to pay.

Do parents need to participate?

Cub Scouts is a family program! Parents are encouraged to attend meetings and activities. We also need volunteers for den leaders and committee roles.

Can we join mid-year?

Absolutely! Registration is open year-round. Scouts can join at any time and jump right into the fun.
