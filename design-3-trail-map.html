Frequently Asked Questions

When do you meet?

Pack 3 meets monthly as a full pack from September through June. Dens meet more frequently based on leader schedules.

What does membership cost?

Annual dues cover BSA registration plus pack activities. Financial assistance is available!

Do parents participate?

Yes! Cub Scouts is a family program. Parents are encouraged to attend and we need volunteers.

Can we join mid-year?

Absolutely! Registration is open year-round. Join anytime!
