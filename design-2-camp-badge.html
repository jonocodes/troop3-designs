Questions?

FAQs

When do you meet?

Pack 3 meets monthly as a full pack from September through June. Individual dens meet more frequently based on den leader schedules.

What does membership cost?

Annual dues cover BSA registration plus pack activities. Financial assistance is available - no scout is turned away!

Do parents need to participate?

Cub Scouts is a family program! Parents are encouraged to attend meetings and activities. We also need volunteers!

Can my child join mid-year?

Absolutely! Registration is open year-round. Scouts can join anytime and jump right into the fun.
