About Pack 3

Nearly a century of building character, citizenship, and adventure in Albany, California.

Our Story

Nearly 90 Years of Scouting in Albany

Albany Cub Scouts Pack 3 was founded in 1935 and has been a cornerstone of youth development in our community ever since. Through generations of dedicated leaders and enthusiastic families, we've helped thousands of children discover the joy of scouting.

Our pack is part of the Golden Gate Council, Herms District, and we're proudly chartered by the American Legion Post 292 of Albany. This partnership has allowed us to maintain a strong presence in the community and provide consistent, quality programming year after year.

Pack 3 has always prided itself on being an inclusive unit that represents the diversity of our community. We are strongly committed to creating a welcoming environment for all children who wish to join our Pack.

The Scout Oath & Law

"On my honor I will do my best to do my duty to God and my country and to obey the Scout Law; to help other people at all times; to keep myself physically strong, mentally awake, and morally straight."

A Scout is: Trustworthy, Loyal, Helpful, Friendly, Courteous, Kind, Obedient, Cheerful, Thrifty, Brave, Clean, and Reverent.
