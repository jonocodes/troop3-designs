About Us

Building Character Since 1935

Albany Cub Scouts Pack 3 has been serving our community for nearly 90 years. We're an inclusive pack that welcomes all kids in kindergarten through fifth grade.

Through camping, hiking, community service, and fun activities, we help children develop character, citizenship, and personal fitness while making lifelong friends.
