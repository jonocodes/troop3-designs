Questions?

When do you meet?

Pack meetings happen monthly from September through June. Dens meet more often based on schedules!

How much does it cost?

Annual dues cover BSA registration plus activities. Financial help is available - no one is turned away!

Do parents come too?

Yes! Cub Scouts is a family program. Parents join meetings and activities - we also need volunteers!

Can we join anytime?

Absolutely! Registration is open all year. Jump in whenever you're ready!
