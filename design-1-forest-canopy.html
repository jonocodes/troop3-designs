Frequently Asked Questions

When do you meet?

Pack 3 meets monthly as a full pack from September through June. Individual dens (grouped by grade) meet more frequently, typically weekly or bi-weekly, based on den leader schedules.

What does membership cost?

Annual dues cover registration with the Boy Scouts of America plus pack activities. Financial assistance is available - no scout is turned away for inability to pay.

Do parents need to participate?

Cub Scouts is a family program! Parents are encouraged to attend meetings and activities with their scouts. We also need volunteers for den leaders, activity helpers, and committee roles.

Can my child join mid-year?

Absolutely! Registration is open year-round. Scouts can join at any time and jump right into the fun.
